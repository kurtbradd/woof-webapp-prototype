Why Is It Important To Pick The Right Breed?

Millions of dogs are brought to shelters every year, many of them are there because the dog breed wasn't a good fit. Our team of professional trainers ensures that wont happen to you, by analyzing your situation and suggesting the breeds that will be best for your lifestyle.

How to pick a breeder?

The ASPCA estimates that over two million dogs are purchased every year through puppy mills. We are tackeling this problem by connecting you with the best dog breeders across North America. They have raised their puppies in nourishing environments and have all passed their breed specfic health tests.
