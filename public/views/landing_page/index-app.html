How are we helping shelters?

In North America there are an estimated 6-8 million dogs & cats brought to shelters every year. It is sad to say that around half of the dogs brought to shelters are euthanized. We are stop the problem at its roots.

Many of the euthanizations could have been prevented. The ones we are working towards stopping are from puppymills and dogs brought to shelters due to a bad match betweens the dog's breed and owner.

We beleive that a professional service connecting invidiuals with the right breed & quality breeders will reduce the burden on the shelters and lessen dog euthanizations

What Is a Woof Certified Breeder?

We know dog's health is important to you. That is why you are not going to get your dog from any breeder. You want your dog from an award winning breeder who has passed their breed specific health tests for their dogs.

Amoung the health tests, you want to make sure that your puppy was raised in welcoming environment that encourages each litter to the right amount of socialization and time with their mom

We screen every breeder that we work with to ensure that you are getting a healthy puppy from a quality breeder.
